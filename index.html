Frequently Asked Questions

What is Christian Minimalism?
Christian Minimalism is the thinnest possible Christian denomination.
What does it teach?
Christian Minimalism defines no doctrine or morals at the structural level. Instead, all theology and ethics is referred to the interpersonal level. When asked what Christian Minimalism has to say about a certain topic in theology or ethics, the answer is undefined.
Why bother?
Individuals are sometimes asked to identify themselves religiously. Christian Minimalism is useful when "Christian" is too broad an answer, but identifying with other denominations is too specific.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Christian Minimalism?
Christian Minimalism is the thinnest possible Christian denomination.
What does it teach?
Christian Minimalism defines no doctrine or morals at the structural level. Instead, all theology and ethics is referred to the interpersonal level. When asked what Christian Minimalism has to say about a certain topic in theology or ethics, the answer is undefined.
Why bother?
Jesus is great. Christian Minimalism is useful for individuals who would like to identify themselves with Jesus while explicitly not committing to any more-specific denomination.

What does undefined really mean?
It means that to get an answer from the person you are talking to, you have to engage them as a person and not as a representative of some larger entity.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Christian Minimalism?
Christian Minimalism is the thinnest possible Christian denomination.
What does it teach?
Christian Minimalism defines no doctrine or morals at the structural level. Instead, all theology and ethics is referred to the interpersonal level. When asked what Christian Minimalism has to say about a certian topic in theology or ethics, the answer is undefined.
Why bother?
Individuals are sometimes asked to identify themselves religiously. In such situations, Christian Minimalism is useful to those for whom "Christianity" is too general a category, but who are not comfortable identifying with any currently-existing denomination.
